I started Feather for you.
I've been mailing doodles to friends and family for years. Some of my favorite moments are their responses after opening a quirky drawing. Last year my wife asked for some pretty obscure Christmas gifts. Since I couldn't find those things I decided to skip the whole 'bad gift exchange' experience and wrap up a few doodles and gift cards instead.
I know I'm not the only one who has trouble giving gifts so I started Feather to help you out.
