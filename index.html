Gifts vs. Gift Cards

Gifts are more than transactions. They show another person that you care. Gift cards are a way of giving another person respect to give them something they will like.

The one downside of a gift card is that it is stripped of emotion. You go to the trouble to buy one but the thought and emotion don't really show through.

Often times a gift shows non-tangibles. That you thought of the person enough to get something on their behalf. So, one of the benefits of a present is the experience of opening up something that's unknown and upon unwrapping you then stare at something shiny and new.

A feather is both a wrapped present of a limited edition art and a gift card. It shows that you are thinking of someone but want them to have something they love.
